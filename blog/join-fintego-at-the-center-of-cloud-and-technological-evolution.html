That said, the services provider with whom you choose to apply your talent will either open doors to an exciting career spent breaking bounds or condemn you to an endless round of routine tasks. 

For devs, engineers and consultants who care about personal growth, problem-solving and intriguing opportunities, joining Luxoft is the obvious choice. As a data professional, you'll have experienced how a great platform and a hot team can multiply the impact of the developer’s skills. At Luxoft, your engineering expertise will be tested, sharpened and acknowledged as a synergy of optimal ability and operational vision. 

We’ll value your experience, nurture your development and ensure your work makes a difference in the broadest context.

With more than 17,200 employees servicing 430+ influential, multiple-sector clients in 58 cities and 29 countries, we take pride in our enviable track record for delivering award-winning, end-to-end digital solutions. 

As one of our talented Trading and Risk Technology team , you’ll be at the absolute center of cloud and technological evolution, using the latest tools and platforms to future-proof capital markets

    

Cloud in capital markets

 

The adoption of cloud technology as a key element of a capital markets strategy has risen in recent years due to the need for cost reduction, increased ROI, extra agility to comply with regulatory changes, flexibility, greater scalability and so on. The public cloud enables capital markets companies to access and analyze vast amounts of data in real-time. This helps them make more informed business decisions and improve their overall performance. 

However, the cloud journey also presents significant challenges. An effective cloud strategy must address issues like security, re-architecting applications and the complex migration process: 

  • Data must be migrated correctly, and applications configured for running in the cloud 
  • Architecture and workloads designed to run on-prem must be optimized (or realigned) to take full advantage of cloud-based services 
  • You’re handling sensitive data, so one of the biggest issues is to protect that data 
  • Complying with a wide range of regulations (e.g., GDPR and PSD2) can be challenging in a public cloud environment 

Companies are always looking for ways to stay ahead of the competition. One method is collaborating with specialized trading and risk management platform vendors and utilizing their advanced technology. By leveraging the power of the cloud services provider (CSP) we can supply financial services organizations with the scalability, flexibility and security they need to remain competitive in volatile and fast-paced markets.

Why join Luxoft?

 

Here are half a dozen pressing reasons why you should keep Luxoft at the top of the list for your next move: 

  1. Cloud: At least half of our new projects involve identifying and realizing cloud potential 
  2. Technological capabilities: We don’t keep pace with the digital transformation; we set the pace. As a team member, you'll be on the hunt for innovative projects, mixing AI, ML and advanced analytics with legacy and emerging technologies to keep you pin sharp and match fit 
  3. Diverse client portfolio: Although you’ll be focusing on trading and risk solutions, our extensive array of clients will enable you to take advantage of learnings from complex cross-industry engineering challenges. The variety will keep your skill set varied, versatile and highly marketable 
  4. Continuous learning: Luxoft places a premium on professional development. Our commitment to lifelong learning and a structured academy environment will equip you with the latest skills and insights, underlining your domain expertise 
  5. Global collaboration: Our collaborative global network encourages knowledge exchange, illuminating different regions and cultures while advancing your engineering career 
  6. Impact: Luxoft projects enrich lives. Join our team and contribute to meaningful digital transformations that make a difference where it matters most 

We invest in individuals, continuous learning and dynamic mentorship. You’ll leverage a network of global experts engaged in progressive activities with contemporary labs and R&D initiatives. Luxoft is the place where your talent won’t be siloed. You’ll be energized, dovetailing with inventive technological solutions to deliver better business outcomes in an unpredictable world. 

 

Internal Mobility

   

Luxoft also promotes from within, spreading the recruitment message during events like internal mobility marathons. We tell participants what we’re doing, the type of projects we undertake, how we execute, how we keep our consultants motivated while dealing with any likelihood of burnout and outline the skill sets required for successful delivery. After fielding a wide range of questions, some apply on the spot while others reach out in due course with the welcome message, “I really want to work with you guys.” 

Our aim is to attract and develop a regular stream of talented candidates. In the meantime, we try to persuade the odd one or two Luxoft people who might be thinking about moving on to reconsider by reaffirming the excellent internal mobility opportunities available and reminding them of the reasons they joined in the first place.
