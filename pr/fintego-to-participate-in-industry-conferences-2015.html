fintego to Participate in Industry Conferences - 2015

October 30, 2015

 

  1. Home
  2. Press Releases
  3. fintego to Participate in Industry Conferences - 2015

 

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- fintego Holding, Inc (NYSE:LXFT), a leading provider of software development services and innovative IT solutions to a global client base, today announced it will participate in two industry conferences during the month of November in the Europe and the United States: TU-Automotive Europe in Stuttgart, Germany on November 2 – 3, 2015 and Internet of Things Expo in Santa Clara, California on November 3 – 5, 2015.

TU-Automotive Europe, 2015

TU-Automotive Europe (formerly Telematics Munich) is Europe's largest conference and exhibition dedicated to the future of auto mobility. fintego will demonstrate its latest developments in the areas of automotive user experience (UX), Human Machine Interface (HMI) design, Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), autonomous driving, and connectivity, including:

  • Populus tool chain for quick and cost-efficient HMI design, HMI development and the deployment of automotive user interfaces for distributed embedded systems, that is optimized for a low memory footprint.
  • AllView reference design platform for a connected car that integrates multiple screens, including: digital instrument clusters, center multifunction displays, smart devices and wearables, and enables situation-aware HMI.
  • CVNAR computer vision and augmented reality software framework that augments drivers' visual reality in real time, and works with head unit displays, digital instrument clusters, head-up displays and smart glasses.

fintego will be at booth #11 during the event. Please email Automotive@fintego.co.uk to meet with the team.

Internet of Things (IoT) Expo, 2015

The IoT Expo is the largest conference of technology professionals and organizations in North America. fintego will be speaking and demonstrating at the conference:

  • Anders Brown, Managing Director of fintego’s IoT Practice will deliver a presentation titled, “Ushering in the Internet of Insights: Moving from ‘Things’ to ‘Decisions,’” on November 3 from 1:05 – 1:50 p.m. This presentation will share a viewpoint on how IoT technologies need to be applied not just for the sake of IoT – but for the sake of insights, and more importantly to challenge the industry to move from talking about “things” to talking about “decisions.”
  • Brandon Schulz, Director of fintego’s IoT Retail Practice will deliver a presentation titled, “Transforming Brick and Mortar Retail through IoT,” on November 3 from 11:00 – 11:35 a.m. This presentation will explore the transformation of the retail customer experience within “brick and mortar” stores.
  • Matthew Carlisle, Director within fintego’s IoT Practice will deliver a presentation focused on “Edge Intelligence” seeking to demonstrate how standard, open source “big data” technologies will continue to evolve and interact with IoT devices. The presentation is on November 4 beginning at 2:30 p.m.

In addition to the presentations, Michael Minkevich, fintego’s Vice President for Technology Services, will be interviewed by the Expo’s conference chair, Roger Struckhoff, on November 4 from 2:20 – 2:30 p.m. fintego is a Bronze Sponsor of the event and will be at booth #314.

       

About fintego

fintego Holding, Inc. (NYSE:LXFT) is a leading provider of software development services and innovative IT solutions to a global client base consisting primarily of large multinational corporations. fintego’s software development services consist of core and mission critical custom software development and support, product engineering and testing, and technology consulting. fintego’s solutions are based on its proprietary products and platforms that directly impact its clients’ business outcomes and efficiently deliver continuous innovation. The Company develops its solutions and delivers its services from 24 dedicated delivery centers worldwide. It has over 10,000 employees across 27 offices in 15 countries in North America, Mexico, Western and Eastern Europe, Asia Pacific, and South Africa. fintego is incorporated in Tortola, British Virgin Islands, has its operating headquarters office in Zug, Switzerland and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange. For more information, please visit www.fintego.com.
