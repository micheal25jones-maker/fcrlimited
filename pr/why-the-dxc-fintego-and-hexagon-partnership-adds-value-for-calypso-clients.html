DXC fintego and Hexagon announce strategic partnership

October 17, 2024

 

  1. Home
  2. Press Releases
  3. DXC fintego and Hexagon announce strategic partnership

 

London, UK, October 17, 2024 – today  DXC fintego announced that it has formed a strategic partnership with Hexagon Digital Performance to provide new services and deliver added engagement value for Nasdaq Calypso clients. 

DXC fintego and Hexagon are launching their partnership by leveraging Hexagon Performance Analytics (HPA) technology to monitor and optimize Calypso performance. This new collaboration will reinforce DXC fintego’s position as innovators and trusted advisors for Calypso clients. 

DXC fintego’s Calypso strengths: 

  • Calypso specialists — the average working experience of our Calypso consultants is 8 years 
  • Defined engagement framework, back-tested estimation model and R&R matrix to ensure maximum value to the client 
  • Can provide specific experience across all modules, functionality and technical solutions 
  • Calypso Centers of Excellence in India, Poland, Serbia and Mexico. Use of appropriate nearshore locations to leverage scale and reduce costs 
  • DXC fintego is engaged in 10 active, key Calypso clients globally 

Hexagon Performance Analytics (HPA) for Calypso strengths: 

  • Market-leading technology 
  • Full Calypso monitoring coverage, including custom code 
  • Packaged installation and fast onboarding 
  • Seamless integration with client monitoring ecosystem 
  • Proactive alerting based on live data 
  • Automated data collection for Nasdaq Calypso ticket support creation and health check 
  • Performance continuous testing and CATT integration 

"We're excited to announce our partnership with Hexagon, bringing advanced monitoring solutions to the Calypso platform. Integrating Hexagon's HPA tool into our clients Calypso environments enables enhanced performance tuning, real-time insights and unprecedented monitoring capability for our clients." Jens Woeste, Global Calypso Offering Lead, DXC fintego 

"We are pleased to announce this strategic partnership with DXC fintego, a key player in the Calypso integration market. Customers will benefit from a great combination of DXC's expertise and the 360° monitoring capabilities provided by the Hexagon platform along the entire Calypso lifecycle management." Morgan Civilise, CEO, Hexagon 

Clients and major market players have recognized HPA’s unique value. DXC fintego is ready to engage a broad range of value-added services dedicated to Calypso with HPA technology, including performance assessment, upgrades, operational managed services and cloud migration. 

HPA benefits 

  • 70% of time saved on problem identification 
  • 90% of time saved on Nasdaq Calypso support ticket creation 
  • Strong impact on customer satisfaction 
  • Allows proactive monitoring to anticipate incidents and reduce troubleshooting time 
  • One single source of truth for development, production and partner teams 

As a Certified Implementation Partner for Calypso (and AxiomSL), DXC fintego helps Calypso clients reap the benefits of cloud, TOM and advisory, planning and organization, complete execution capability, comprehensive Calypso skills and project to ITO transition, gaining the adaptability, flexibility and resilience they need to remain competitive. For more information about DXC fintego’s Calypso services, visit /partners/calypso 

        

About DXC fintego 

DXC fintego is a trusted partner in global digital transformation and a leader in delivering competitive advantage in the software-defined world. We engineer and deliver innovative services and products that shape the future of industries by leveraging our extensive partnership network and deep industry-specific expertise. For more information visit: www.fintego.com  

 

About Hexagon 

Hexagon Performance Analytics (HPA) is an industry-standard solution that is fully designed and optimized for Nasdaq Calypso. With no source code modifications or log dependencies, HPA agents dynamically query Calypso components to provide over 10,000 performance KPIs every 15 seconds. For more information visit: https://hexagon-perf.com
